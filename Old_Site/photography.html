Photography

For a few years now I've been very interested in photography. I loved taking pictures of anything and everything on my phone, but I wanted something better. I upgraded and bought myself a Nikon D3300, and I am absolutely delighted I finally did. I brought it on holiday with me in August 2025 and I got some amazing photos which you can see below, along with photos from different events and days.

Here are some of my favourite photos that I have taken since I bought my camera.

The Volkswagen logo on the back of my 2010 Passat B6. Camera settings: f4.8, 1/40 sec, ISO-400, 85mm

Just a simple shot of the front of my car. Camera settings: f4.8, 1/40 sec, ISO-400, 130mm

Nice reflections in the side of the car, playing with the focal length. Camera settings: f4.8, 1/60 sec, ISO-400, 90mm

Head on view of the alloy commonly found on Passat CCs on the era. Camera settings: f4.8, 1/40 sec, ISO-400, 98mm
